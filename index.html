How to Connect Your TV to Streaming Platforms

This guide explains how to install and sign in to popular streaming apps on your TV.

Step 1 — Connect TV to Internet

  1. Open Settings on your TV
  2. Go to Network / Wi-Fi
  3. Connect to your home internet
  4. Confirm connection

Step 2 — Open the TV App Store

Use your TV’s app store to search for streaming apps.

Step 3 — Install Streaming Apps

  • Netflix, YouTube, Prime Video, Disney+
  • Hulu, Peacock, Paramount+, Max
  • Apple TV, ESPN, Tubi, Pluto TV

Step 4 — Sign In

  1. Open the app
  2. Select Sign In
  3. Enter email and password

Step 5 — Use “Enter Your Code”

Some apps show a code. Visit the website shown on TV, sign in, and enter the code.
